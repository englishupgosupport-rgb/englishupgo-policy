Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 8, 2026

Welcome to EnglishUP GO. This Privacy Policy outlines how we manage and protect the information related to your use of our mobile application.

1. Information Handling

EnglishUP GO is designed to respect your privacy. Here is how we handle data:

  • No Personal Accounts: We do not require registration, login, or any personal information (such as your name or phone number) to use the app.
  • Local Storage: Your learning progress, "Favorites" list, and quiz statistics are stored locally on your mobile device. We do not have access to this data, and it is not sent to our servers.
  • Local Notifications: The app uses device-based reminders to help you stay on track with your studies. These notifications are handled entirely by your operating system.

2. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with reliable third-party providers for functionality and monetization:

  • Google Play Services: Used to manage app installation and updates.
  • Google AdMob: To keep the app free, we may display ads. AdMob may use device identifiers to provide relevant advertising content and analyze ad performance.

3. Children’s Privacy

EnglishUP GO does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. We are committed to complying with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

4. Data Security

Since your data lives on your phone, its security depends on your device settings. If you delete the app or clear its cache, your local progress will be permanently removed.
